About Us

Horizon Synergy is a youth-led innovation company based in South Africa. We build apps, games, and culture-driven digital experiences rooted in real value and purpose. Tech meets creativity. Code meets community.

Our Mission

To design and deliver tech experiences that are creative, inclusive, and built for impact — while creating space for African youth to lead in tomorrow's digital world.
